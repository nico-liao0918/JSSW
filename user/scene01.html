The Foundational Scene.

This scenario is not part of any book or scenario I am writing about. This is a separate writing story or scenario.

The current year of this is 2025.

I am the author of this and yet I am also the character in this scenario.

I was given a Wishing Stone in 2017. Since then I have not used it until 2022.

My wishes will be given in depth in the Backstory Scene later on. But my wishes are Money, Pre Fabricated Inventions, Protection, Success, and Pheromones.

When the Wishing Stone was given to me. I was still in the hospital with my Father in the hospital bed. A man came to me and said that you need this more than I do. And I was in the Restroom at the time washing my hands. I accepted the package without asking what it is for. When I was done. I turned and he was no longer there. I rushed out but there was no one there. And plus. It was night. Like around 12 midnight. There was little staff but when I asked no one knew about where he went or who he was. So. I kept it in my bag for safekeeping.

Thus. The end of the Foundational Scene.
